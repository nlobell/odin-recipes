Easy Apple Cobbler


This easy Apple Cobbler Recipe makes a perfect fall dessert. There is nothing quite like that warm apple filling, topped with a buttery

crust and finished off with a creamy scoop of ice cream. If you love apple desserts as much as I do, then you are going to love this

quick and easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Â¼ cup water

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Combine 3 tablespoons sugar and

    cinnamon in a bowl.

  2. Layer apples in an ungreased 9x13-inch baking pan, sprinkling each layer with

    cinnamon-sugar.

  3. Combine flour, 1 cup sugar, egg, and baking powder with a fork. Sprinkle over apples.

    Lightly sprinkle water over top.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is golden brown, about 30 minutes.
